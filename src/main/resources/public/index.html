  5. Details about unmanned aerial activities

    The self declaration below does only apply for applicants of activities that fall under the Swiss ordinance on aircraft of special categories1, article 15, 16 and 17: model aircraft (including mini drones); kites and unmanned tethered balloons/blimps; unmanned free balloons and sky lanterns with a volume of more than 1m3.

      By filing this request, the applicant for unmanned aircraft operations or other unmanned aerial activities, declares that:
    • she/he is familiar with the rules and regulations in the ordinance on aircraft of special categories particularly with the possible requirement for a third party liability insurance according to article 20 and with possible cantonal rules and regulations which are based on article 19
    • these aerial activities do not conflict with current national laws and regulations regarding protection of data privacy, as well as regarding protection of military installations.
    • she/he has taken actions necessary so that no third parties on ground are endangered by aerial activities under her/his control.
